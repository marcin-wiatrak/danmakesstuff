We produce unique tables based on wood, epoxy and metal. The main target of our manufacture is creating genuine state-of-art products with the utility implementation. Projects that exceed the limits of imagination and- what some would think - the limits of capabilities is what fascinate us the most. Let's create together a table tailored for the 21st century.

Who stands behind it all?

Howdy! Ny name is Daniel Otfinowski. I am young man entering adulthood with great passion. Wishing to make as many people as possible happy via my designs. My adventure with craftmanship initiated when I was only 12 years old. This is when I started producing my very first creations made of wood and metal. The beginnings were difficult, but bit by bit my designs had started gaining an interest and admiration among my closests and others. At that point- yet, I had no idea this would turn into a massive part of my existence and my life-long passion. For as long as I can remember - nature has always been my source of inspiration. My friends were often surprised when looking at a simple piece of wood I used to state it was a piece of art. Currently, I try to make the most of my time focusing on craft. Every single item I create, becomes something very special to me. The visible joy and happiness of people whom I have pleasure creating for is constantly giving me a drive to further development of my passion.

What makes us stand out?

Why choice of the right table is so important?
